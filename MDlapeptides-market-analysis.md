True North (our brand partner) is added as the rightmost column in every table above, benchmarked against the US-made market.

**The takeaway:** Across the catalog True North sits within or above the US-made range on most items (🟢/🔵), which is healthy. But on the tirzepatide line especially, they undercut even the cheapest established US-made vendor. As a new brand, there's no upside to being cheaper than the legitimate US market — it erodes margin and signals "budget" before the brand has equity. Recommendation: hold or raise the 🔴 items to at least match the cheapest US-made peer. The grey-market comparison is context only — True North should not price against Chinese-API vendors.
